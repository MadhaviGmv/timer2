enter(item, eta=None, priority=0)¶

Enter item into the scheduler.

Parameters:
  • item – Item to enter.
  • eta – Scheduled time as a datetime.datetime object.
  • priority – Unused.
  • callback – Callback to call when the item is scheduled. This callback takes no arguments.
